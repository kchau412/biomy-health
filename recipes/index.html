A quick note

What makes a recipe low FODMAP?

Low FODMAP eating is usually less about removing all flavour and more about making smarter swaps. Think garlic-infused oil instead of garlic cloves, the green tops of spring onions instead of onions, lactose-free dairy where needed, and portion-aware use of ingredients that can be tolerated in smaller amounts.

Biomy recipes are designed to be practical, culturally inclusive, and grounded in the reality that people do not just eat grilled chicken and sad rice forever.

Everyone’s triggers are different. These recipes are a starting point, not medical advice.
