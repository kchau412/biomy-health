Privacy

Plain English. No legalese.

Biomy is an early-stage, solo-built project. We want to be completely transparent about what we collect, what we don't, and why.

Last updated: April 2026.

What we collect.

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your primary condition
  • Your areas of interest

That's it. Collected via our waitlist form and stored securely using Tally.

What we do not collect.

  • Your location or IP address
  • Medical records, prescriptions, or clinical history
  • Payment information
  • Data from third-party sources or brokers
  • Biometric data of any kind

Health data.

We use your condition only to personalise your early access experience and understand who Biomy is being built for. Never shared. Never used for ad targeting. Never sold.

A reminder. Biomy is a wellness platform, not a medical service. Nothing on this site constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Your rights.

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct any inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data at any time
  • Unsubscribe at any time, every email includes a link

Who we are.

K

Biomy is a solo project built by Karan. No large team, no data department, no investors with access to your information. Questions? hello@biomy.health

Changes to this policy.

We may update this policy as Biomy evolves. Material changes will be communicated by email to everyone on the waitlist.
